About Us

Who we are

Vision

To become a leading hub for information, analysis and response on the Climate-Conflict Nexus in the ASAL region.

By bridging the gap between data, policy, and action, we strive to build a resilient society that thrives in the face of environmental adversity.

Mission

To Avail Early warning and Response initiatives on Climate and Conflict, empowering Stakeholders and Communities to take timely and informed action against climate-induced Conflicts and Shocks in the ASAL regions.

Our Mandate

  • Conducting detailed research to uncover the root causes and impacts of climate change on conflicts.
  • Delivering timely analyses to inform policymakers, humanitarian actors, and other stakeholders.
  • Advocating for inclusive, gender-sensitive approaches to resilience-building and peace initiatives.
  • Developing practical frameworks that integrate climate resilience into local, national, and regional agendas.
  • Promoting early warning systems to detect emerging risks and enable preventive actions.
  • Raising awareness and fostering knowledge-sharing on the intersection of climate change and conflict.

Our Impact

Since our inception, CeCCA has:
  • Built a strong foundation of data and knowledge on climate-induced conflicts across Kenya.
  • Supported local institutions in enhancing their capacity for conflict prevention and resilience building.
  • Developed innovative early warning tools to track and respond to emerging climate-related risks.
  • Collaborated with regional and international partners to integrate climate resilience into peace and development agendas.

A Network for Change

CeCCA’s efforts are deeply rooted in collaboration. By bringing together diverse stakeholders—from government entities to civil society organizations—we create a unified front against the challenges posed by climate change.

As part of a broader national effort, CeCCA draws inspiration from and contributes to global and regional frameworks addressing the nexus of climate and conflict.

Our Work and Priority Areas

At the Centre for Conflict and Climate Action (CeCCA), we are dedicated to addressing the critical challenges posed by climate-induced conflicts across Kenya and beyond.

Looking Ahead

CeCCA is steadfast in its commitment to creating a peaceful, sustainable future for communities grappling with the challenges of climate-induced conflicts.

Programs

What we do offer

Research and Analysis

Through in-depth studies, research and analysis, we provide data-driven insights into the complex dynamics between climate change and conflict.

Capacity Building and Advocacy

Through training programs and advocacy efforts, we empower communities, governments, and organizations to address climate-induced conflicts sustainably.

Policy Development

Working closely with governments, NGOs, and international bodies, we contribute to the formulation of policies that integrate climate and conflict considerations.

Early Warning Systems

Our Early Warning Systems leverage data and technology to detect emerging conflicts and climate threats, enabling proactive response.

Awareness and Knowledge Sharing

We create platforms for dialogue, learning, and collaboration among researchers, policymakers, and the public.

Climate Mitigation Measures

We encourage and promote the use of renewable energy sources solar and windpower and efficient energy use practices.
