Welcome to Center for Conflict and Climate Action

CeCCA - Committed to reducing climate-induced conflicts

Vision: To become a leading hub for information, analysis and response on the Climate-Conflict Nexus in the ASAL region by bridging the gap between data, policy, and action, we strive to build a resilient society that thrives in the face of environmental adversity.

Mission: To Avail Early warning and Response initiatives on Climate and Conflict, empowering Stakeholders and Communities to take timely and informed action against climate-induced Conflicts and Shocks in the ASAL regions.
